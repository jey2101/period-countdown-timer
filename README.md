# Period Countdown Timer

A simple, browser-based countdown timer for teachers to display live time remaining in each class period — no installation, no login, no app to download.

**[Open the app →](https://jey2101.github.io/period-countdown-timer/)**

---

## What It Does

The app shows a card for each period in your school day. Each card displays:

- The period's **start and end time**
- A **live countdown** to when the period ends
- The **current period** is highlighted in green with a "Now" badge
- Periods that have ended are shown in gray
- The current time and date are displayed at the top of the page

## Setup

**First time only — takes about 2 minutes:**

1. Open the app: [jey2101.github.io/period-countdown-timer](https://jey2101.github.io/period-countdown-timer/)
2. Click the **⚙️ gear icon** in the top-right corner to open Settings
3. Set the number of periods in your school day (default is 9)
4. Enter your period times — choose one of two methods:

### Option A — Auto-Calculate (easiest)
Click the **Auto-Calculate** tab and enter:
- Your **first period start time** (e.g., 8:00 AM)
- How long each **period is** in minutes (e.g., 50)
- How long **breaks between periods** are in minutes (e.g., 5)

The app calculates all end times for you. Click **Apply Times**.

### Option B — Manual Entry
Click the **Manual Entry** tab and type in the start and end time for each period individually. Click **Save Times**.

**Your settings are saved automatically** — you only need to set them up once. They'll still be there the next time you open the app, even after closing the browser.

## Tips for Classroom Use

- **Bookmark the page** on your classroom computer or interactive whiteboard for quick access
- **Keep the tab open** all day — the timers update automatically every second
- Works on any device: desktop, laptop, tablet, or phone
- No internet connection is needed after the page first loads
- To display on a projector or TV, just open the page in full-screen mode (press **F11** on most computers)

---

## Changing Your Schedule

If your schedule changes (e.g., early release day or a special schedule), open Settings with the ⚙️ gear icon and update the times. Changes take effect immediately.

---

## Privacy

This app runs entirely in your browser. No data is sent to any server — your period times are stored only on your own device.
